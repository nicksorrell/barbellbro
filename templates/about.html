You're using Barbell Bro! I made this simple and free app after realizing that sometimes you don't feel like doing math to load up a barbell while at the gym, especially on pre-workout! I figure others could use a bro on their phone to help out too.

I hope you find Barbell Bro as useful as I do for spending less time number-crunching and more time lifting big.
